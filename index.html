Interactive Design 1 Project

Final Prototype


Our assignment was to come up with an original app idea and develop it into a medium fidelity prototype. Testing our app along the way.


Before working on the prototype we had to do research on potential competitors and interviews with Subject Matter Experts. These questions included:

  • Are there any music apps that you use?
  • How do you find out about concerts in your area?
  • How do you make plans for a weekend?
  • Are there any apps you use to help plan events?
  • What are things that you look for in mobile apps?
  • What are some struggles when finding an event/concert?
  • When making plans with friends how do you share event details?

Things we discovered

  • Most users looked for apps that are easy to use and that are intuitive.
  • When going out for the weekend they make little plans and tend to “go with the flow”.
  • Only hear about popular bands through major music streaming sites.
  • Some users were a little confused on what the app provided.
